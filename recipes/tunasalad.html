Tuna Salad

Description

The perfect sandwich filling for a half-lazy person.

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Mayo
  • Onions
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Greens (optional)
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop ingredients into desirable sizes
  2. Combine all ingredients into a bowl
  3. Season to taste
  4. Serve
