Creamy Garlic Chicken

Description

I found this video and tried doing everything Chef told me to do in the video. My first attempt turned out fine so I kept making it whenever I had some cream and a chicken layin' around.

Ingredients

  • Cream
  • Garlic
  • Chicken
  • Greens
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Mushrooms
  • Chicken broth

Steps

  1. Fry chicken
  2. Remove chicken from pan
  3. Fry mushrooms
  4. Fry greens
  5. Fry garlic
  6. Add chicken broth
  7. Let broth reduce
  8. Add chicken back to pan
  9. Add cream
  10. Add seasoning
  11. Serve
