Creamy Garlic Chicken

Ingredients

  • Cream
  • Garlic
  • Chicken
  • Greens
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Mushrooms
  • Chicken broth

Steps

  1. Fry chicken
  2. Remove chicken from pan
  3. Fry mushrooms
  4. Fry greens
  5. Fry garlic
  6. Add chicken broth
  7. Let broth reduce
  8. Add chicken back to pan
  9. Add cream
  10. Add seasoning
  11. Serve
